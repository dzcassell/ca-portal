Firefox setup

Firefox may use its own certificate store. If Firefox still shows certificate errors after OS setup, import the certificate directly into Firefox.

Download Firefox Helper Download Certificate Only
  1. Open Firefox.
  2. Go to about:preferences#privacy.
  3. Scroll to Certificates.
  4. Click View Certificates.
  5. Open Authorities.
  6. Click Import.
  7. Select the downloaded certificate.
  8. Check Trust this CA to identify websites.
