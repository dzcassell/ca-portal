Android setup

Android behavior varies by version, browser, and app. Some apps do not trust user-installed CA certificates.

Download Certificate

  1. Download the certificate.
  2. Open Settings.
  3. Search for Install a certificate or CA certificate.
  4. Select CA certificate.
  5. Choose the downloaded certificate.
  6. Name it something recognizable, such as {{ company_name }} Cato Root CA.
  7. Restart Chrome or your browser.
